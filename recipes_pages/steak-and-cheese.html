Steak & Cheese Subs

Description

This is a recipe that will make three sandwiches, so I would suggest sharing with friends. In terms of preparations, it doesn't take too long to prepare. It is also highly customizable as you can put all kinds of sauces and have it with a multitude of sides. I would recommend mayonnaise and the fajita mix in the sandwich. I would also recommend fries, vegetables, and/or chips as sides.

Ingredients

  • 2 Gary's Quicksteak Boxes
  • Shredded Mozzarella
  • Italian Sub Roll - Recommended but you can put it on any bread you want.
    • The following ingredients are optional.
  • Sliced Fajita Mix
  • Baby Bella Mushrooms

Steps

  1. Brown the meat as directed on the box. Season the meat with salt & pepper to taste.
  2. This step is optional. It depends on your tastes. Saute the mushrooms and/or fajita mix. Season with salt & pepper to taste.
  3. Sprinkle in the mozzarella on the meat and wait for it to melt.
  4. Put the steak & cheese in the bread of your choice along with anything you want & enjoy!
