General Tso Chicken Balls

Description

I will admit, this recipe was one that I threw together haphazardly one day. Yet, the taste is something to die for. It takes minimal preparation and attention to make it. I did not use peanuts when I made and took pictures of the final product simply because I didn't have it on hand. Peanuts is not required but it will make the dish tasty.

Ingredients

  • TGI Friday's Boneless Chicken Buffalo Bites aka Chicken Balls

  • 1 cup of rice
  • Olive Oil
  • 1 cup of water
  • Tsang's General Tso Sauce

  • Peanuts

Steps

  1. Get a rice cooker or pan and coat the bottom with non-stick cooking spray then pour in the rice, coat it in olive oil, and pour in one cup of water. For people with pan in hand, I will recommend bringing the water to boil and then letting it simmer until rice is tender.

  2. Put the balls in the air fryer at 380 degrees Fahrenheit for 10 minutes.

  3. Cut up the balls and put it in a pot along with the sauce and peanuts.

  4. Put it all in a bowl. Enjoy!
