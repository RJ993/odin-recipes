Sweet Asian Chicken

Description

This recipe is a favorite of mine. It has a good mix of spiciness and sweetness with a buttery undertone. Pair it with a little bit of salt and it is one of the tastiest things on this planet. The sauce can be found in Wal-Mart although I would imagine that it could be found somewhere else. Most of the metric in the ingredients will depend on how many people you feed, so if you're only feeding yourself, I would recommend choosing the lower end and you may have leftovers after you are done.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Thighs
  • 1 or 2 cups of rice
  • Olive Oil
  • 1 cup of water
  • Sweet Asian Chili Sauce

  • 3 or 5 tablespoons of butter

Steps

  1. Get a rice cooker or pan and coat the bottom with non-stick cooking spray then pour in the rice, coat it in olive oil, and pour in one cup of water. For people with pan in hand, I will recommend bringing the water to boil and then letting it simmer until rice is tender.

  2. Spray a pan with non-stick cooking spray and put the chicken in seasoned with salt & pepper and garlic paste. Have it at high heat for 4-5 minutes. You may have to flip the chicken halfway through to ensure it doesn't stick to the pan. Bring it to low heat for 10 minutes. Flip the chicken a few times during the 10 minutes.

  3. Cut up the chicken, put in the sauce and butter. Keep the heat on and keep stirring until all is mixed together.

  4. Put the rice in the bowl along with your chicken and enjoy!
